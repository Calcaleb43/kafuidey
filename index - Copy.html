How To MC Any Event

Every day, Masters of Ceremonies are engaged to host corporate and social events. Since there is little published material about the ins and outs of the profession, even the most experienced MCs run into problems. Veteran Corporate MC Kafui Dey provides a needed guide to the work of a Master of Ceremonies with How To MC Any Event.

The basic components of every event

What makes an event successful

The 11 qualities you need to make an event planner choose you as MC

How to draw up a running order at short notice

How to thank speakers and make them feel appreciated

9 qualities of an outstanding master of ceremonies

What to do when disaster strikes

How to avoid these 5 common mistakes

What you need to know and manage during any event

Kafui Dey

Author & Publisher

Kafui Dey gave up a 17-year sales career to chart a new path as Ghana's first full-time Corporate Master of Ceremonies.

To date he has emceed over 300 events ranging from conferences, product launches, awards ceremonies, diplomatic functions and book launches.

A frequent seminar speaker, Kafui Dey is currently the host of GH Today, the award-winning breakfast show on GHOne TV in Ghana. He also writes a weekly column for Business & Financial Times.
